About me

I was 'proficient' in Microsoft FrontPage (RIP), and had a GeoCities website or ten back in the early-mid 2000's. Like most people, I modified my MySpace page in a very tasteful manner! My biggest achievement was when the design of my personal website Tragicness, (I was a teenager) using chromeless windows got me invited to host on a private domain.

I then did nothing for 15> years and find myself starting from scratch. The internet looks very different these days, especially when it comes to accessibility. The possibilities seem truly endless with code, and I find that prospect really exciting!
